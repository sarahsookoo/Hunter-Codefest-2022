Create the Number Game

In this game, you will be given 5 numbers. You will have to use all of the first 4 numbers along with the five mathematical operations (+, -, * , /, %) to create the final number. Enter the solution in the order it should be solved in without parentheses and with spaces in between each number and operation. Try to solve them as fast as possible, while also not getting it wrong, as that adds a 10 second penalty. How fast can you correctly solve 4 problems?
